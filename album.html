No Mind by Yama Hachi

“No Mind” by Yama Hachi is an ambient album that explores the delicate balance between tranquility and chaos. Inspired by Japanese minimalism and the struggle with anxiety, it combines soothing melodies with subtle distortions to reflect the melting away of inner peace.

Tracklist

  1. "Fractured Silence"
  2. "Echoes of Calm"
  3. "Sakura in Decay"
  4. "Shadows Over The Water"
  5. "The World Melts Away"

Meet the Band

Haruto Mori and Akio Takahashi

Akio Takahashi and Haruto Mori collaborated on No Mind as a deeply personal project to explore the universal struggle of finding peace in an ever-disturbed world.
